About Gym Titus

Supporting student wellness through simple access to recreation resources.

Mission

The mission of Campus Recreation is to aid all students in their pursuit of health and wellness by reducing stress and meeting students’ needs as they pursue academic success.

Vision

Help students access the gym at the comfort of their homes through clear information, sign‑ups, and training resources.

About Us

We are students improving the experience we noticed needed attention — simple, secure, and accessible information for campus recreation.
